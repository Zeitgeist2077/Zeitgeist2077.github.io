Legendary Characters by Faction

The Emperor of Mankind & Malcador

The God-Emperor of Mankind – Immortal ruler of humanity. Creator of the Primarchs. Mortally wounded by Horus 10,000 years ago and sustained on the Golden Throne (technically alive but a rotting corpse).

Malcador the Sigillite – The Emperor's closest friend and Regent of Terra. Master psyker. Died during the Siege of Terra after sacrificing himself to power the Golden Throne.

Loyalist Primarchs

Roboute Guilliman – Ultramarines. Alive. Current Imperial Regent.
Lion El'Jonson – Dark Angels. Alive. Recently returned.
Jaghatai Khan – White Scars. Missing, presumed lost in the Webway.
Leman Russ – Space Wolves. Missing after entering the Eye of Terror.
Rogal Dorn – Imperial Fists. Presumed dead during the Scouring.
Sanguinius – Blood Angels. Dead. Slain by Horus during the Siege of Terra.
Ferrus Manus – Iron Hands. Dead. Killed by Fulgrim.
Vulkan – Salamanders. Alive (Perpetual).
Corvus Corax – Raven Guard. Alive but transformed into a shadow creature.

Traitor Primarchs

Horus Lupercal – Dead. Slain by the Emperor.
Fulgrim – Emperor's Children. Alive as Daemon Prince of Slaanesh.
Perturabo – Iron Warriors. Alive as Daemon Prince.
Angron – World Eaters. Alive as Daemon Prince of Khorne.
Mortarion – Death Guard. Alive as Daemon Prince of Nurgle.
Magnus the Red – Thousand Sons. Alive as Daemon Prince of Tzeentch.
Konrad Curze – Night Lords. Dead. Assassinated.
Lorgar Aurelian – Word Bearers. Alive as Daemon Prince.
Alpharius Omegon – Alpha Legion. Status unknown (possibly dead).

Adeptus Astartes Chapter Masters

Marneus Calgar – Ultramarines. Alive. Veteran of many wars.
Dante – Blood Angels. Alive. Over 1,200 years old.
Logan Grimnar – Space Wolves. Alive. Great Wolf.
Helbrecht – Black Templars. Alive. Zealous crusader.

Adeptus Mechanicus

Belisarius Cawl – Archmagos Dominus. Alive. Creator of the Primaris Marines.
Thulia Ghul – Notable Tech-Priestess known for her work on ancient technologies.
Kelbor-Hal – Former Fabricator General of Mars. Traitor during the Heresy.

Inquisition

Gregor Eisenhorn – Radical Inquisitor. Alive (though heavily augmented).
Katarinya Greyfax – Puritan Inquisitor. Alive.

Chaos Gods

Khorne – God of Blood and War.
Tzeentch – God of Change and Sorcery.
Nurgle – God of Plague and Decay.
Slaanesh – God of Excess and Pleasure.

Chaos Champions

Abaddon the Despoiler – Warmaster of Chaos. Alive.
Khârn the Betrayer – Champion of Khorne. Alive.
Ahriman – Chief Librarian of the Thousand Sons. Alive.

Orks

Ghazghkull Mag Uruk Thraka – Prophet of the Waaagh!. Alive. Greatest Ork Warboss.

Necrons

Szarek the Silent King – Supreme ruler. Alive.

Tyranids

Drukhari

T'au Empire
