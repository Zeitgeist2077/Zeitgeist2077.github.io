The Primarchs

Loyalist Primarchs
  • Roboute Guilliman – Ultramarines, Lord Commander of the Imperium (returned).
  • Lion El'Jonson – Dark Angels, recently awakened.
  • Jaghatai Khan – White Scars, lost in the Webway.
  • Leman Russ – Space Wolves, disappeared after the Heresy.
  • Rogal Dorn – Imperial Fists, presumed dead defending Terra.
  • Sanguinius – Blood Angels, slain by Horus (legendary martyr).
  • Ferrus Manus – Iron Hands, killed by Fulgrim.
  • Vulkan – Salamanders, believed to be immortal.
  • Corvus Corax – Raven Guard, transformed into a shadow creature.
Traitor Primarchs (Daemon Princes)
  • Horus Lupercal – Warmaster, slain by the Emperor.
  • Fulgrim – Emperor's Children, Daemon Prince of Slaanesh.
  • Perturabo – Iron Warriors, Daemon Prince of Chaos Undivided.
  • Angron – World Eaters, Daemon Prince of Khorne.
  • Mortarion – Death Guard, Daemon Prince of Nurgle.
  • Magnus the Red – Thousand Sons, Daemon Prince of Tzeentch.
  • Konrad Curze (Night Haunter) – Night Lords, assassinated.
  • Lorgar Aurelian – Word Bearers, Daemon Prince.
  • Alpharius Omegon – Alpha Legion, status unknown (twin primarchs).
Other Legendary Characters
  • Abaddon the Despoiler – Current Warmaster of Chaos.
  • Marneus Calgar – Chapter Master of the Ultramarines.
  • Dante – Chapter Master of the Blood Angels.
  • Saint Celestine – Living Saint of the Adepta Sororitas.
  • Szarek the Silent King – Ruler of the Necrons.
  • The Swarmlord – Apex Tyranid warrior.
