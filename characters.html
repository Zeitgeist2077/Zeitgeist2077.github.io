Primarchs

Roboute Guilliman

Ultramarines

Revived in the 41st millennium, Guilliman serves as Lord Commander of the Imperium. A master of logistics and statecraft, he struggles to preserve humanity in an age of superstition.

Lion El'Jonson

Dark Angels

Recently awakened, the Lion stalks the galaxy hunting Chaos. He is secretive, calculating, and utterly relentless.

Leman Russ

Space Wolves

The Emperor’s executioner vanished into the Eye of Terror, seeking a way to return in humanity’s darkest hour.

Rogal Dorn

Imperial Fists

Master of fortification and defence, Dorn disappeared after the Heresy, leaving only legend behind.

Sanguinius

Blood Angels

The angelic Primarch who gave his life fighting Horus. His sacrifice still inspires the Imperium.

Vulkan

Salamanders

A perpetual who cannot die, Vulkan values humanity above all and will one day return.

Corvus Corax

Raven Guard

Warp-altered and shadow-like, Corax hunts Chaos from within the darkness itself.

Jaghatai Khan

White Scars

Master of speed warfare, the Khan vanished into the Webway pursuing Drukhari raiders.

Ferrus Manus

Iron Hands

Killed early in the Heresy, his death reshaped his Legion into cold machine devotees.

Horus Lupercal

Warmaster

Once the Emperor’s favoured son, Horus led the greatest rebellion in history before his death.

Fulgrim

Slaanesh

Corrupted by perfection, Fulgrim became a Daemon Primarch of excess and decadence.

Perturabo

Iron Warriors

A bitter siege master who turned to Chaos and now rules as a Daemon Prince.

Angron

Khorne

Driven by rage and the Butcher’s Nails, Angron lives only for bloodshed.

Mortarion

Nurgle

Enduring endless suffering, Mortarion became the Plague Lord’s champion.

Magnus the Red

Tzeentch

A tragic sorcerer who now serves the Chaos God of change and magic.

Konrad Curze

Night Lords

A terror-driven Primarch who embraced fear as justice and accepted his death.

Lorgar Aurelian

Word Bearers

The first to worship Chaos, Lorgar’s faith ignited the Horus Heresy.

Alpharius / Omegon

Alpha Legion

Masters of deception. Their true fate and loyalty remain unknown.

Imperium of Man

The Emperor of Mankind

God-Emperor

The immortal ruler of humanity, now bound to the Golden Throne. Though physically broken, his psychic power guides and protects mankind across the galaxy.

Malcador the Sigillite

Regent of Terra

The Emperor’s closest confidant and one of the most powerful psykers to ever live. He sacrificed himself to sustain the Golden Throne during the Siege of Terra.

Belisarius Cawl

Adeptus Mechanicus

An ancient Archmagos responsible for creating the Primaris Space Marines. His knowledge spans ten thousand years of forbidden technology.

Marneus Calgar

Ultramarines

Chapter Master of the Ultramarines and one of the Imperium’s greatest commanders, now reborn as a Primaris Marine.

Dante

Blood Angels

The oldest living Space Marine, Dante has led the Blood Angels for over a millennium and now rules Imperium Nihilus.

Logan Grimnar

Space Wolves

The Great Wolf of the Space Wolves, known for his ferocity, honour, and refusal to bow to Imperial politics.

High Marshal Helbrecht

Black Templars

Fanatical leader of the Black Templars, leading endless crusades across the galaxy in the Emperor’s name.

Gregor Eisenhorn

Inquisition

A once-puritan Inquisitor who turned radical, using forbidden tools to fight Chaos while slowly becoming what he hunts.

Katarinya Greyfax

Inquisition

A devout and uncompromising Inquisitor, known for her psychic resistance and hatred of heresy.

Chaos Champions

Abaddon the Despoiler

Black Legion

Warmaster of Chaos and heir to Horus. He has launched multiple Black Crusades against the Imperium.

Khârn the Betrayer

Khorne

The most feared champion of Khorne, Khârn lives only for slaughter and has killed allies and enemies alike.

Ahzek Ahriman

Thousand Sons

Former Chief Librarian of the Thousand Sons, now cursed by his own Rubric, endlessly seeking forbidden knowledge.

Typhus

Death Guard

Champion of Nurgle and harbinger of plagues, spreading disease across the galaxy.

Lucius the Eternal

Slaanesh

A duelist cursed to be reborn through those who take pride in killing him.

Xenos

Ghazghkull Mag Uruk Thraka

Orks

The greatest Ork warboss in the galaxy, Ghazghkull leads massive Waaaghs! and believes himself chosen by the Ork gods.

Szarek, The Silent King

Necrons

The last ruler of the Necrons, returned to unite his people and reclaim the galaxy from younger races.

Trazyn the Infinite

Necrons

An obsessive collector of history, Trazyn steals entire armies and relics to preserve them in his galleries.

Asdrubael Vect

Drukhari

The supreme overlord of Commorragh, ruling through manipulation, cruelty, and absolute power.

Lelith Hesperax

Drukhari

The deadliest gladiator of the Dark City, unmatched in speed and precision in combat.

Commander Shadowsun

T'au Empire

The greatest military commander of the T’au, master of ranged warfare and strategic planning.

Aun'Va

T'au Ethereal

The spiritual leader of the T’au Empire, guiding their expansion through the philosophy of the Greater Good.

The Swarmlord

Tyranids

A hyper-intelligent Tyranid commander that evolves with every battle, leading the Hive Mind’s greatest invasions.

Chaos Gods

Khorne

God of War

Khorne is the Chaos God of rage, war, and bloodshed. He cares not from where the blood flows, only that it flows.

Tzeentch

God of Change

Master of fate, magic, and manipulation, Tzeentch weaves endless schemes across time.

Nurgle

God of Decay

The Plague God represents decay and disease, but also resilience and twisted rebirth.

Slaanesh

God of Excess

Born from the fall of the Aeldari, Slaanesh embodies obsession, pleasure, and perfection taken to ruinous extremes.
