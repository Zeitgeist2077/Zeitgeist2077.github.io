Legendary Characters of the 41st Millennium

The God-Emperor of Mankind

The immortal Master of Mankind. Born in prehistoric Anatolia, he guided humanity for millennia. He created the Primarchs and the Space Marine Legions. During the Horus Heresy he was mortally wounded by his favoured son Horus. For 10,000 years he has sat on the Golden Throne, sustaining the Imperium and powering the Astronomican with his psychic might.

Loyalist Primarchs

Roboute Guilliman – Primarch of the Ultramarines. Master statesman and strategist. Resurrected in the 41st millennium, he now serves as Lord Commander and Imperial Regent, fighting to save the Imperium from collapse.

Lion El'Jonson – Primarch of the Dark Angels. Recently awakened after millennia of slumber. A master hunter and tactician.

Jaghatai Khan – Primarch of the White Scars. Lightning-fast warrior who disappeared into the Webway.

Leman Russ – Primarch of the Space Wolves. The Wolf King vanished into the Eye of Terror after the Heresy.

Rogal Dorn – Primarch of the Imperial Fists. Heroic defender of Terra during the Siege.

Sanguinius – Primarch of the Blood Angels. The Angel, slain by Horus. His sacrifice remains one of the most tragic moments in Imperial history.

Ferrus Manus – Primarch of the Iron Hands. Killed by Fulgrim during the Heresy.

Vulkan – Primarch of the Salamanders. A Perpetual who cannot truly die.

Corvus Corax – Primarch of the Raven Guard. Transformed himself into a creature of shadow and vengeance.

Traitor Primarchs

Horus Lupercal – Warmaster of the Imperium turned traitor. Leader of the Horus Heresy. Slain by the Emperor.

Fulgrim – Primarch of the Emperor's Children. Corrupted by Slaanesh and became a Daemon Prince.

Perturabo – Primarch of the Iron Warriors. Master of siege warfare and now a Daemon Prince.

Angron – Primarch of the World Eaters. Transformed into a raging Daemon Prince of Khorne.

Mortarion – Primarch of the Death Guard. Lord of Plague and Daemon Prince of Nurgle.

Magnus the Red – Primarch of the Thousand Sons. Mighty sorcerer and Daemon Prince of Tzeentch.

Konrad Curze – Primarch of the Night Lords. The Night Haunter, assassinated after the Heresy.

Lorgar Aurelian – Primarch of the Word Bearers. First to embrace Chaos.

Alpharius Omegon – Primarchs of the Alpha Legion. Their true fate remains a mystery.

Chapter Masters & Imperial Heroes

Marneus Calgar – Chapter Master of the Ultramarines. Lost both arms to a Tyranid Hive Tyrant and replaced them with master-crafted power fists. Veteran of countless campaigns.

Dante – Chapter Master of the Blood Angels. Over 1,200 years old. Wears the Death Mask of Sanguinius and is one of the most respected warriors in the Imperium.

Logan Grimnar – Great Wolf of the Space Wolves. Wields the mighty Axe Morkai.

Helbrecht – High Marshal of the Black Templars. Zealous crusader who swore to hunt Abaddon.

Tu'Shan – Chapter Master of the Salamanders. Known as the protector of his people.

Orks

Ghazghkull Mag Uruk Thraka – The Prophet of the Waaagh!. Greatest Ork Warboss alive. Believes he is chosen by Gork and Mork. Has survived multiple "deaths" and continues to grow stronger with each battle.

Nazdreg Ug Urdgrub – Bad Moon Warboss famous for using advanced (looted) technology.

Necrons

Szarek the Silent King – Supreme ruler of the Necrons. Awakened his people to combat the Tyranid threat after 60 million years of slumber.

Trazyn the Infinite – Eccentric collector who preserves history through stasis fields and theft.

Orikan the Diviner – Master chronomancer and seer.

Tyranids

The Swarmlord – The pinnacle of Tyranid evolution. A supreme hive commander capable of rapid adaptation to any threat.

Old One Eye – Legendary Carnifex that has survived countless battles across many hive fleets.

Drukhari

Asdrubael Vect – Supreme Overlord of Commorragh. Rose from nothing through cunning, betrayal, and political mastery.

Lelith Hesperax – Undefeated Wych Queen and the greatest arena fighter in Commorragh.

T'au Empire

Commander Shadowsun – Supreme military commander. Master of stealth and kauyon (the Patient Hunter) strategy.

Aun'Va – The Ethereal Supreme. Spiritual leader of the Greater Good philosophy.
