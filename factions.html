Adeptus Astartes - Space Marine Chapters

The Emperor's finest superhuman warriors.

  • Ultramarines - Codex-compliant, disciplined leaders.
  • Dark Angels - Mysterious, hunt traitors and the Fallen.
  • Blood Angels - Artistic but plagued by the Black Rage.
  • Space Wolves - Savage, anti-authority Fenrisian warriors.
  • Imperial Fists - Unyielding siege masters.
  • White Scars - Lightning-fast bike and speed warfare.
  • Raven Guard - Stealth, ambush, and guerrilla tactics.
  • Salamanders - Humanitarian flame experts from Nocturne.
  • Iron Hands - Cybernetically augmented, emotionless.
  • Black Templars - Fanatical crusading chapter.
