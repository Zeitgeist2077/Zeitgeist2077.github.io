Major Factions

Imperium of Man

Human Empire

The Imperium is a galaxy-spanning empire of humanity, ruled by the immortal God-Emperor from the Golden Throne. It is vast beyond comprehension, containing millions of worlds, yet it is crumbling under its own weight. Constant war, corruption, and religious fanaticism define daily life.

Despite its technological stagnation, the Imperium survives through sheer scale, faith, and sacrifice. Billions die every day to hold the line against alien threats and the horrors of Chaos.

Sub-factions:
  • Adeptus Astartes – Genetically engineered super-soldiers created from the Primarchs.
  • Adeptus Mechanicus – Tech-priests who worship the Machine God and preserve ancient technology.
  • Astra Militarum – The Imperial Guard, vast armies of ordinary humans.
  • Adepta Sororitas – Fanatical warrior nuns devoted to the Emperor.
  • Inquisition – Secret police rooting out heresy, aliens, and corruption.
  • Imperial Navy – Controls space warfare and fleet combat.

Space Marine Chapters

Genetically Enhanced Warriors

The Adeptus Astartes are humanity’s ultimate warriors, created using the genetic material of the Primarchs. Each chapter has its own traditions, culture, and combat doctrine.

Though they number only around a million in total, their impact on the battlefield is unmatched. They act as shock troops, heroes, and living legends.

Notable Chapters:
  • Ultramarines – Masters of discipline and strategy.
  • Dark Angels – Secretive hunters of the Fallen.
  • Blood Angels – Noble but cursed by genetic flaws.
  • Space Wolves – Savage warriors from Fenris.
  • Imperial Fists – Masters of defence and siege warfare.
  • White Scars – Lightning-fast hit-and-run fighters.
  • Raven Guard – Experts in stealth and ambush.
  • Salamanders – Protectors of humanity.
  • Iron Hands – Obsessed with cybernetic perfection.
  • Black Templars – Zealous crusaders.

Chaos

Forces of the Warp

Chaos is the manifestation of emotion and corruption within the Warp. It is ruled by four dark gods who feed on mortal souls and ambition.

The forces of Chaos include traitor Space Marines, daemons, and cultists. Their goal is the destruction or domination of reality itself.

Major elements:
  • Chaos Space Marines (Black Legion, World Eaters, Death Guard, Thousand Sons)
  • Daemons of the four Chaos Gods
  • Cultists and traitor armies

Orks

Waaagh!

Orks are a brutal, fungal species that live only for war. Their society is driven by strength, and the biggest Ork becomes the boss.

They possess a strange psychic field that makes their belief shape reality—red vehicles go faster simply because Orks believe it.

When enough Orks gather, they form a massive war crusade known as a Waaagh!, capable of devastating entire sectors.

Aeldari

Ancient Psychic Race

Once the dominant species in the galaxy, the Aeldari fell into decadence, accidentally creating the Chaos God Slaanesh.

Now scattered and dwindling, they fight to survive extinction.
  • Craftworld Aeldari – Disciplined survivors living on massive ships.
  • Drukhari – Cruel raiders who feed on suffering.
  • Harlequins – Mysterious performers serving the Laughing God.
  • Ynnari – Followers of the god of the dead, seeking salvation.

Tyranids

Hive Mind Swarm

The Tyranids are an extragalactic species controlled by a single Hive Mind. They consume all biological matter to fuel their endless evolution.

Entire planets are stripped bare, leaving nothing but lifeless rock.

Each invasion is led by evolving bioforms like the Swarmlord.

Necrons

Ancient Machines

Once a dying organic race, the Necrons transferred their consciousness into immortal metal bodies.

Now awakening after millions of years, they seek to reclaim the galaxy they once ruled.

Their technology is far beyond anything humanity possesses.

T’au Empire

The Greater Good

A young and rapidly expanding empire, the T’au believe in unity and cooperation under the philosophy of the Greater Good.

They rely heavily on advanced technology, drones, and ranged combat instead of brute strength.

Leagues of Votann

Kin

Descended from ancient human colonists, the Kin are clone-based and guided by ancient AI known as Votann.

They are master craftsmen, miners, and warriors with extremely advanced technology and strong traditions.
