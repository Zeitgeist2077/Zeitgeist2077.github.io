Key Events & Concepts

The Great Crusade

Age of Expansion

The Great Crusade was a galaxy-wide campaign led by the Emperor to reunite lost human worlds. With the Primarchs and Space Marine Legions, humanity conquered vast regions of space.

It was an age of hope, reason, and technological advancement—before everything fell apart.

The Horus Heresy

Galaxy-Shattering Civil War

Horus, the Emperor’s favoured son, turned to Chaos and led half the Space Marine Legions in rebellion.

The war nearly destroyed humanity. It ended with Horus’ death and the Emperor being mortally wounded and placed on the Golden Throne.

The Golden Throne

Life Support of the Emperor

The Golden Throne keeps the Emperor alive and allows him to guide humanity through the Warp.

It also powers the Astronomican, a psychic beacon that enables faster-than-light travel.

The Imperium Today

Endless War

Ten thousand years after the Heresy, the Imperium is a decaying empire locked in constant war.

Technology is barely understood, religion dominates society, and survival comes at an immense cost.

The Warp & Chaos

Realm of Madness

The Warp is a parallel dimension of pure emotion and psychic energy.

From it come the Chaos Gods—Khorne, Tzeentch, Nurgle, and Slaanesh—who manipulate reality and corrupt mortals.

The Primarchs

Demigod Generals

The Primarchs are the Emperor’s sons, each created to lead a Space Marine Legion.

Some remained loyal, while others betrayed humanity and became Daemon Princes.

The Xenos Threat

Aliens Everywhere

Humanity faces countless alien threats including Orks, Tyranids, Necrons, and Aeldari.

Each poses a unique danger, from endless war to total biological extinction.

The Great Rift

Galaxy Split in Two

A massive warp storm tore the galaxy in half, creating Imperium Nihilus—cut off from the Emperor’s light.

This event plunged humanity into its darkest age yet.
