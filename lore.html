The Grimdark Lore of the 41st Millennium

It is the 41st Millennium. For more than a hundred centuries the Emperor has sat immobile on the Golden Throne of Terra. He is the master of mankind by the will of the gods, and master of a million worlds by the might of his inexhaustible armies. He is a rotting carcass writhing invisibly with power from the Dark Age of Technology...

Ancient & Major Historical Events

The War in Heaven (c. 60 million years ago)

A cataclysmic conflict between the Old Ones and the Necrontyr (who became the Necrons). The Old Ones created the Aeldari and Krork (proto-Orks) as living weapons. The Necrons, allied with the star gods known as the C'tan, nearly wiped out the Old Ones. This war scarred the galaxy and led to the Necrons entering stasis for millions of years.

The Great Crusade (M30)

The Emperor's campaign to reunite humanity after the Age of Strife. The Primarchs were rediscovered and led their Legions across the stars in the Emperor's name.

The Horus Heresy (M31)

The greatest betrayal in human history. Warmaster Horus, corrupted by Chaos, turned half the Space Marine Legions against the Emperor. The civil war devastated the Imperium and left the Emperor mortally wounded on the Golden Throne.

The 13th Black Crusade (M41–M42)

Abaddon the Despoiler's greatest campaign. The destruction of Cadia opened the Great Rift, splitting the galaxy in two and allowing Chaos to pour into realspace.

Detailed Faction Lore

Imperium of Man

The largest empire in the galaxy, ruled in the Emperor's name. It is a brutal, superstitious regime where billions die every day to sustain the Emperor and the war machine. Technology is treated as holy relic. Key events: Horus Heresy, War of the Beast, and the ongoing Indomitus Crusade led by Guilliman.

Chaos

The eternal enemy. The Chaos Gods (Khorne, Tzeentch, Nurgle, Slaanesh) dwell in the Warp and constantly corrupt mortals. The Horus Heresy was their greatest victory, and the opening of the Great Rift their most recent triumph.

Orks

Brutal, fungal xenos created as weapons during the War in Heaven. They live only for war. Ghazghkull Mag Uruk Thraka's current massive Waaagh! is one of the greatest threats facing the Imperium in the 41st millennium.

Aeldari

Ancient psychic race whose empire fell due to hedonism, birthing the Chaos God Slaanesh. Now scattered across Craftworlds, Commorragh (Dark Eldar), and the Ynnari. They fight desperately for survival.

Tyranids

Extragalactic hive fleets that consume all biomass. The arrival of Hive Fleet Behemoth in 745.M41 marked the beginning of the Tyrannic Wars that continue to ravage the Eastern Fringe.

Necrons

Ancient robotic dynasty that sacrificed their souls for immortality. They ruled the galaxy 60 million years ago and are now awakening from tomb worlds. The Silent King's return is a major turning point in the current era.

T'au Empire

A young, optimistic empire guided by the Greater Good. They expand rapidly using advanced technology and diplomacy. Their first major clash with the Imperium was the Damocles Gulf Crusade.
