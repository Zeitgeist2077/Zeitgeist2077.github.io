The Grimdark Lore of the 41st Millennium

It is the 41st Millennium. For more than a hundred centuries the Emperor has sat immobile on the Golden Throne of Terra. He is the master of mankind by the will of the gods, and master of a million worlds by the might of his inexhaustible armies.

Key Historical Events

The Horus Heresy

The great betrayal. Half the Space Marine Legions turned to Chaos under Warmaster Horus. A civil war that nearly destroyed the Imperium. The Emperor was mortally wounded and placed on the Golden Throne.

The Great Crusade

The Emperor's campaign to reunite humanity after the Age of Strife. The Primarchs were created and led the Legions to conquer the galaxy.

The War of the Beast

Orks nearly destroyed the Imperium after the Heresy. The Beast was one of the greatest Ork threats in history.

The 13th Black Crusade

Abaddon’s latest and most successful invasion of Cadia, which led to the opening of the Great Rift (Cicatrix Maledictum).

Faction Lore Highlights

Imperium of Man

A decaying, theocratic empire of a million worlds. Humanity clings to survival through faith, ignorance, and endless war. Technology is revered as holy and innovation is often heresy.

Chaos

The realm of the Chaos Gods exists in the Warp. They constantly corrupt mortals and seek to consume realspace. The Great Rift has split the galaxy in two.

Orks

Created as a warrior race by the Old Ones. They grow stronger through fighting. Their psychic "Waaagh!" energy makes their ramshackle technology work.

Aeldari

Once ruled the galaxy but fell due to hedonism that birthed Slaanesh. Now scattered: Craftworlds, Commorragh raiders, and Harlequins.

Tyranids

Hive fleets from another galaxy. They consume all biomass to create more Tyranid organisms. The Shadow in the Warp disrupts psykers.

Necrons

Ancient race that gave up their souls for immortality in metal bodies. Now awakening from 60 million years of slumber to reclaim the galaxy.

T'au Empire

A young, optimistic empire that believes in the Greater Good. They expand rapidly using advanced technology and diplomacy.
